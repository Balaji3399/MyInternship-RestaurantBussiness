About Us

Our Story

At RoyalSpice, we believe that every meal is an opportunity to create cherished memories. Our journey began with a passion for the rich and diverse flavors of Indian cuisine. We set out to offer our patrons an authentic taste of India in a setting that radiates warmth and elegance.

Our Cuisine

Our kitchen is a realm of creativity and tradition, where our expert chefs masterfully craft each dish using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients. From the sizzling tandoori delights to the fragrant curries and sumptuous biryanis, our menu is a gastronomic journey through the subcontinent.

Why We are Special ?

Indian Flavours


At RoyalSpice, we take pride in serving truly authentic Indian cuisine, prepared with traditional recipes and genuine Indian spices.

Elegant Ambience


The restaurant's ambiance offers a blend of elegance and comfort. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner setting or a relaxed family gathering, our welcoming atmosphere caters to various occasions.

Friendly and knowledgeable Staff


Our staff is dedicated to providing an exceptional dining experience. They are well-versed in our menu offerings and are always ready to assist and guide you through your culinary journey.

Customizable Spice Levels


We understand that everyone has their own preference when it comes to spice levels. At RoyalSpice, you have the freedom to customize the heat in your dishes.

Specialty Dishes


We offer a selection of specialty dishes unique to RoyalSpice. These are chef's creations that you won't find anywhere else.

Sustainable and Local Sourcing


At RoyalSpice, we are committed to sustainability and supporting local communities. We source many of our ingredients locally, ensuring freshness and reducing our environmental footprint.

Our Services

Dine-In

Enjoy an exquisite dining experience in our welcoming restaurant. Immerse yourself in our cozy ambiance as you savor our chef's culinary creations.

Takeaway or Delivery

Can't make it to our restaurant? No worries! Take advantage of our convenient takeout or delivery service. Indulge in your favorite dishes from the comfort of your home or office.

Order Online

Experience the convenience of ordering your favorite meals with a simple phone call. Browse our menu, make your selections, and place your order for pickup or delivery over the phone.
